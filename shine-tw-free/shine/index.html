About Us

ONE OF THE PRIMARY OBJECTIVES OF THE CITY IS TO TRANSFORM ITSELF INTO A SMART SUSTAINABLE CITY.

TO ACHIEVE SUCH MILESTONE, THE CITY, AMONG OTHERS, IS JUMPSTARTING THE ICT COMMUNITY THROUGH COLLABORATIVE EFFORTS BETWEEN THE CITY GOVERNMENT AND THE PRIVATE SECTOR, SUCH AS THE PAMPANGA CHAMBER OF COMMERCE (PAMCHAM).

THE PAMPANGA BUSINESS + INNOVATION SUMMIT IS DESIGNED TO CREATE AWARENESS REGARDING TODAY'S TECHNOLOGY.

BY BRINGING TOGETHER A DIVERSE GROUP OF INDUSTRY LEADERS, INNOVATORS, ENTREPRENEURS, AND PROFESSIONALS, WE AIM TO INSPIRE BUSINESS OWNERS AND ALIKE TO DIGITIZE AND TRANSFORM THEIR BUSINESSES AND ORGANIZATION TO CONFORM WITH TODAY'S TECHNOLOGICAL ADVANCEMENTS.
