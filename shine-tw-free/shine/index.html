Business Innovation Summit

About Us

In celebration of its 20th Anniversary, the PamCham and the City Government of San Fernando, Pampanga are hosting the first Pampanga Business + Innovation Summit, which is intended to be one of the biggest ICT events in Pampanga, with over fifty (50) participating exhibitors and partners.

General Objectives

  • Exhibition of leading businesses and tech companies locally and internationally
  • Networking opportunities with industry leaders and innovators
  • Panel discussions on technology, innovation, and business growth
  • Engagement with renowned business owners in Pampanga and in the Philippines
  • Facilitation of international partnerships and generation of new business opportunities
